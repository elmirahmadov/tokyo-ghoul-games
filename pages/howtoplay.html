About Tokyo Ghoul

Tokyo Ghoul is a manga series written and illustrated by Sui Ishida. The story follows the survival struggles of humans in Tokyo, where ghouls (monstrous creatures) and humans are at war with each other.

The protagonist, Kaneki Ken, becomes part ghoul after a ghoul attack, leading him to face new challenges and meet various characters like the Quinx Squad, who are both allies and enemies in his fight for survival.
